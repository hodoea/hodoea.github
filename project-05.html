European Innovation Academy

I attended a summer program abroad called the European Innovation Academy. This is a program where studenter from several countries come together for 3 weeks and try develop an innovativ IT-service. I participated in a group with students from Netherlands, Austria and Slovakia. We came up with a market platform where the goal was to connect large travel groups with local companies that offer group activities. One of the group members already had a company where he offered group activities for locals. I designed this landingpage to promote the service. And it was also developed a prototype in php by one group member.
