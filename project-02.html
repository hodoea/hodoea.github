Skatteetaten

In 2012 I and a studyfriend attended an user experience competition by Bekk AS and the Norwegian Tax agency. We came to the finale along with two other swedish teams. The problem statement for the finale was as following: What is the solution for creating more engaging and simpler services for the population who are using the Tax agencys services? I designed the prototype in Adobe photoshop. And you can also find the presentation file in the right column.
